Hyperspectral Images

automatically classifying hyperspectral images

I developed a system to automatically classify hyperspectral images as subjects the navy was interested in: sale, concrete, water, wakes, and vegetation. I programed my own implementations of KPCA, SVD, and the nearest neighbor classifier.

This program needed to run in real time. Due to the size of our data, and the year, 2012, KPCA took a long time to run. I decreased the runtime and rate of false positives by identifying eleven wavelengths (down from 111) that were most important for identifying each category of spectra through correlation.

2012, Naval Research Lab
