Bye, SF

May 2021

San Francisco has always felt like home. Even before I moved here four years ago, I was here almost every school break. I’ll miss how easy it is to cook when all the ingredients are already so tasty, the beach to the mountain tops to the city in just 30 minutes. I’ll miss the bus lines: 22 to Sunday morning dance parties and crafts over tea, 33 to the best banh mi in the city, live jazz, and Golden Gate Park, 48 to the thrift store where most of my clothes are from, and, controversially, my favorite burritos.

I’ll miss knowing what I want in a home: a lemon tree in my backyard, a pottery studio down the street, and close friends who will sit with me.

I’ll miss all of you, who are the real reason why San Francisco has always felt safe and warm. The past month has been a whirlwind of adventures, gently renewed connections, and new friends. Thank you for being with me as I try and stumble and learn and grow.

Katherine Lee, misc ↩︎
