Hello!

I’m currently a Research Engineer at Google Brain Research in San Francisco, CA.


I’m broadly interested in building machine learning systems we can trust. This means figuring out when models are untrustworthy, what knobs change their behavior, and developing interactions and visualizations to learn where strengths and weaknesses of models are.

Previously, I had great fun learning about how real neural networks (brains!) process their surroundings with Jonathan Pillow and Uri Hasson during my undergrad in Operations Research at Princeton.

You can find me on the internet: Twitter, Google Scholar
